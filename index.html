Join the East End in helping raise funds for Multiple Sclerosis Research!

This May we’re participating in The May 50k which calls on participants to leave their limits behind and run or walk 50km during the month.

We love a tight deadline though so we’re leaving it to the last minute and cramming it all into the last day of the month. We love the Adelaide East End too and don’t want to leave so we’re just going to run around the block...235 times.

Why MS? As a small team with two members living with MS it’s important to us rally behind them and show our support. By lapping the block every few minutes for six hours we’re hoping to bring visiblity to an otherwise invisible illness.

From 11am come see us on the Ebenezer Place - Rundle Street - Union Street block and make a donation, or visit 235laps.com and follow the link to donate online.

Thanks,
Liv, Dan and Scott
